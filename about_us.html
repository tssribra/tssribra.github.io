About Us

Restaurant 1903 opened in 2011 and located near the Sothern cross railway station in Melbourne CBD.

We are opened Monday to Saturday from 9.00 am to 10.00 pm and offered specials meals lunch and dinner. Our chefs are highly experienced and offer a unique highly satisfying and delicious find dining experience.
